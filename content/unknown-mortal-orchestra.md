<p>Unknown Mortal Orchestra blends art pop, indie rock, and neo-psychedelic influences into a unique and captivating sound. Tracks like "Not in Love We're Just High," "Multi-Love," and "So Good at Being in Trouble" showcase their dreamy and genre-defying approach to music. These songs, released between 2013 and 2018, remain some of their most celebrated works.</p>
