<p>Tom Pettys timeless rock anthems and heartfelt lyrics continue to resonate with fans worldwide. From classics like "Free Fallin" and "I Wont Back Down" to introspective gems like "Square One" and "Jack," his music remains a pillar of American rock. This collection of tracks spans his illustrious career, culminating in the 2019 release of "American Girl" on a definitive hits album.</p>
